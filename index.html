About


Our goal at RJ Designs Unlimited is to help create our customers’ dream kitchen while exhibiting excellent customer service. We strive to finish each job with quality workmanship and in a timely manner, resulting in 100% customer satisfaction. We are located in Central Florida, for your remodeling needs!

RJ Designs Unlimited is a local family owned and operated kitchen remodeling business. After years of being traditional handymen, Rich and Dave decided to start their own kitchen remodeling business in 2006. With 10 years of professional experience under their tool belt they have what it takes to turn any kitchen into a dream kitchen!
